His dark materials is a trilogy consisting of the books, The northern lights, the subtle knife and the amber spy glass. It is about a alternate universe where a lot of things are different. One of things is that in this universe your soul is outside your body this is your dæmon. dæmons can take any form they want while you are a child but when you get older it settals on its final appearance. it could be as a wolf or a butterfly, it dosent matter. It follows the young lyra as she embarks on a adventure that would not only lead her to the people that hav been stealing kids from her town but also talking polerbares, gypses and much more. In the story lyra plays a important part in the future of her universe as she is special and is one of the few who can read the special alethiometer. in the storys she meets will a boy from our universe who accidentally gets sucked into theirs. He holds the special subtle knife which cuts the very fabric of time and lets him travle through universes.
